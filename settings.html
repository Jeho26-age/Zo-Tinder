About Zo-Tinder

Zo-Tinder hi Mizo thalaite tana liau liauva siam a ni a. He app hi thian thar siam nan te, mahni nena mizia inmil zawng hmuh chhuah nan te, leh in-date hial thlenga hman theih tura duan a ni.

Social media khawvel kan luh thuk tawh em avang hian, thian thar siam hi a chang chuan a lo harsa tawh thin a. Chuvang chuan, Zo-Tinder hian thalaite inbiak pawh zung zungna leh inhmel-hriat tawnna kawng awlsam zawk a rawn hawn kan beisei a ni.

Mimal Thuchah & Solo Developer:
He app hi Solo Developer (mi pakhat chauh) kutchhuak a ni a. Thil chhuanawm tak pakhat chu, he app pumpui hi Mobile Phone chauh hmanga siam a ni. Mi pakhat chauh kutchhuak a nih avangin, tuna app lian tak takte anga features neih vek chu thil theih rih loh a ni a. Nimahsela, hma sawn zel tura beih a ni a, hun lo la kal zel turah features thar leh hlawkthlak tak tak rawn dah belh zel tum a ni.
"Thian thar hmu la, i hmalam hun nuam zawk chu vawiin atang hian tan rawh."
